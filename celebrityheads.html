Celebrity Heads Game

Challenge your friends to guess which celebrity they are by asking yes or no questions. The game is fun, fast-paced, and perfect for group play.

How to Play

  • Point the phone at a player to swap their head with a celebrity's image.
  • The player cannot see their new head, but everyone else can.
  • The player must guess the celebrity using only 'Yes' or 'No' questions.
